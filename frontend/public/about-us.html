    • about us:

    Team Ivy formed for a computing project for Simon Fraser University's CMPT 433 - Embedded Systems course. Their goal was to make botanism an achieveable goal for anyone. Their focus was creating a quick, easy way to monitor the status of any plant. With Ivy, anyone who desires is able to grow and maintain their plants. Vegetables, flowers, or houseplants -- the choice is yours!
